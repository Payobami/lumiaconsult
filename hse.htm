Company Policy On Safety

The management of Lumia - mayes construction company limited has dedicated itself to providing the necessary active leadership and support in order to develop and maintain a progreamme to ensure the security, protection, and well – being of personnel and company property by the prevention and control of physical violence, and misconduct, theft or sabotage.

Management believes that working conditions for every employee including sub-contractors should be made safe by eliminating hazard and by safety education. Good safe practices are a part of our operations procedure. Most accidents are the results of mistake and can be corrected, that is by training, motivating, planning and supervision. Nothing takes the place of good common sense and taking the time to plan your work adequately.

The company recognizes and accepts its responsibility to provide safe and healthfu; working conditions. Complete cooperation with this vital program is expected of every employee and subcontractor of Lumia - mayes construction company limited.
